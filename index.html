About

Flame Institute and Therapy is a multifaceted agency dedicated to empowering individuals and communities. We offer tailored mentorship programs to help clients achieve their personal and professional goals. Our compassionate counselors provide insightful guidance to navigate life's challenges. We deliver leadership training to equip individuals with essential skills for career excellence and organizational impact. Additionally, our spiritual services foster inner peace and fulfillment. With a holistic approach, Flame Institute and Therapy stands as a beacon of hope and transformation for those seeking to improve their lives and reach their full potential.

1. Mission

To Impact Society through Counselling, Training, Mentorship, Consultancy and Research.

2. Vision

A Global-standard, Generational-relevant, Community centered Training and Therapy Institute.

3. Core Values

  1. Family: A belief that the Family is the cornerstone of any society and has to be taken care of to have any progress and stability.
  2. Leadership: Society falls and rises with leadership, thus stressing on strategic, servant, and spiritual leadership.
  3. Academics: Education is the pedestal on which greatness is attained and should be a route towards other attainments in the economy including entrepreneurship, innovation, sports, and health.
  4. Ministry: Church and the subsequent service attached to it are key balancing and stabilizing factors of any society, without which morals and ethics find no place of exercise.
  5. Empowerment: It's by creating conducive conditions that humanity is given capacity to progress and prosper.

Family

This faculty will desire to reach the family to empower it for stability. The following services will be offered:

  • Parenting Talks
  • Couples Therapy
  • Premarital Counselling
  • Media Talks
  • Family Literature
  • Family Conflict Resolution

Leadership

The target groups will be secular and spiritual leaders in all institutions and organizations. The services will include:

  • Institutional Leaders Training
  • Organizational Leaders Training
  • Governance Leaders Training
  • Leadership Literature
  • Media Programs
  • Strategic Mentorship

Academics

This will be the educational wing of the institute. The following are the projected services as God provides an opportunity:

  • Academic Counselling
  • Academic Talks
  • Computer Training

Ministry

Programs

Explore our various accreditation programs designed to empower and educate individuals across different sectors.

Ministry Accreditation Program (MAP)

This program will target church leaders and ministers.

Couples Accreditation Program (CAP)

This program will target couples that are preparing for marriage and those that are already married.

First Ladies Accreditation Program (FLAP)

This program will target spouses of ministers - Bishops, Apostles, Revs, Pastors and Assistant Pastors.

Leadership Accreditation Program (LAP)

This program targets emerging leaders in all sectors of society - Ministry, Academics, Business, Governance.

Computer Package Training

Comprehensive training on computer packages including MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and more.

Short Courses

Various short courses designed to provide targeted skills and knowledge in a short duration.
