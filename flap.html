First Ladies Acredation Program(FLAP)

This program will target spouses to Ministers - Bishops, Apostles, Revs, Pastors and Assistant Pastors. It will be a 6 month class program on the Major Issues that Pastoral Spouses have to deal with in their day to day ministry.

  • Month 1 - Personal Calling
  • Month 2 - Ministry Roles / Duties
  • Month 3 - Boundaries / Standards
  • Month 4 - Self Care/ Grooming
  • Month 5 - Personal Development
  • Month 6 - Life Balance
  • Month 7 - Finances / Investments
  • Month 8 - Entrepreneurship
  • Month 9 - Ministerial Relationships
  • Month 10 - Women Ministry
  • Month 11 - Children & Youth ministry
  • Month 12 - Introduction to Counselling

Join our comprehensive 12-month First Ladies Accreditation Program (FLAP) at Flame Institute and Therapy, tailored specifically for the spouses of Ministers, including Bishops, Apostles, Reverends, Pastors, and Assistant Pastors. This program offers valuable insights and practical tools to navigate the unique challenges of ministry life. Enhance your ability to support your spouse, develop personally and professionally, and effectively contribute to your ministry community. Through engaging classes and hands-on activities, you'll build a network of like-minded individuals, gain confidence in your roles, and find balance in your personal and ministerial life. Join us to empower yourself and make a lasting impact in your ministry.
